Frequently Asked Questions

How do I integrate my race data with Motorlytics?

Motorlytics is a web-based platform with an easy to use interface.

Is Motorlytics suitable for amateur racers?

Absolutely! Motorlytics is designed for racers of all levels. Our platform is particularly valuable for amateur and club-level racers who don't have access to expensive data analysis tools. The insights gained from correlating setup, weather, and performance can significantly improve your racing without requiring extensive technical knowledge.

Can I track multiple cars or racing categories?

Yes, Motorlytics allows you to manage multiple cars under a single account with a premium subscription. Each car can be associated with different racing categories, setups, and historical data. This makes it perfect for teams or drivers who compete in multiple categories or who upgrade their vehicles throughout the season.

How accurate is the weather data integration?

Motorlytics uses high-precision weather APIs that provide accurate historical and real-time weather data for racing circuits worldwide. This includes temperature, humidity, wind conditions, and track temperature estimates.

Does Motorlytics work offline at the track?

No, our platform requires an internet connection to function.
