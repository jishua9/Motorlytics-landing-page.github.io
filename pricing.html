Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between the Pro and Team plans?

The Pro plan is designed for individual competitors who want to get the edge on their competitors, while the Team plan is intended for race teams mnaging multiple drivers and vehicles. The Team plan includes features like team management dashboard and setup sharing across team.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. You can manage your subscription through your account settings.

Do you offer a free trial?

We do not offer a free trial. However, you can try our service for 14 days and cancel anytime if you're not satisfied.
