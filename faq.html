Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Motorlytics and how it can help improve your racing performance.

General Questions

What is Motorlytics?

Motorlytics is an all-in-one race management platform designed to help drivers and teams track performance, manage maintenance, and optimize race day operations. The platform connects your car setup, weather conditions, and race results to help you make data-driven decisions.

Who is Motorlytics for?

Motorlytics is designed for everyone in motorsport - from amateur club racers to professional race teams. Whether you're managing a single car or a fleet of race vehicles, our platform scales to meet your needs with appropriate pricing options.

Do I need technical knowledge to use Motorlytics?

Not at all! Motorlytics is designed with a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to input and analyze your data. No coding or technical background is required. We also offer comprehensive tutorials and support to help you get started.

Features & Functionality

How does the setup correlation feature work?

Our setup correlation feature stores your complete car setup for every session at every track. It allows you to track detailed setup records, access track-specific history, save setup templates, and log session-by-session changes. This creates a powerful knowledge base that grows with each race weekend, helping you find the optimal setup for specific tracks and conditions.

What weather data does Motorlytics provide?

Motorlytics automatically records detailed weather data for every session, including temperature (ambient and track), humidity, pressure, wind speed/direction, and precipitation. The platform also provides forecasting for upcoming events and can match current conditions with similar historical conditions to guide your setup decisions.

How does parts lifecycle management help me?

Our parts lifecycle management allows you to track every component on your race car, monitor wear, schedule maintenance, and understand how parts aging affects performance. This helps prevent failures, ensures your winning setup can be replicated precisely, and keeps your car in peak condition.

Can I import my lap times and race results?

Yes! Motorlytics allows you to easily import session results from timing services to automatically populate your performance data. This can then be analyzed in relation to your setup and weather conditions to build a comprehensive understanding of what works for your car and driving style.

Pricing & Plans

Is there a free version of Motorlytics?

Yes, we offer a free Amateur plan perfect for club racers. This includes a single driver account, one vehicle profile, basic maintenance tracking, event calendar, and basic weather forecasts. You can upgrade to our Pro or Team plans anytime to access more advanced features.

What features are exclusive to the Pro and Team plans?

Pro subscribers get multiple vehicle profiles, advanced part tracking, full service history, detailed lap analytics, customizable setup templates, and setup comparison tools. The Team plan adds multiple driver accounts, unlimited vehicle profiles, team management dashboard, and custom driver reports.

Can I change my subscription plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. When upgrading, you'll immediately get access to all the features of your new plan. If you downgrade, you'll maintain access to your current plan until the end of your billing cycle.

Data & Privacy

Is my racing data secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure your information remains private and protected.

Who owns the data I input into Motorlytics?

You retain full ownership of all data you input into the platform. We do not share your data with third parties without your explicit consent. You can export or delete your data at any time.

Can I share my setup data with teammates?

Yes, the Team plan allows multiple driver accounts with controlled access to shared data. You can choose what information is shared across the team while maintaining privacy for sensitive data.

Support & Resources

How do I get help if I have questions?

We offer multiple support channels including documentation, tutorials, email support, and live chat for Pro and Team subscribers. All users have access to our comprehensive knowledge base and community forums.

Can I request new features?

Absolutely! We welcome feature requests and continuously improve the platform based on user feedback. You can submit suggestions through our feedback portal or contact our support team directly.
